Strawberry Green Tea Crepe Cake

Description

A strawberry green tea crepe cake filled with fresh cream and strawberries!!

Ingredients

  • 4 egg
  • 185g cake flour
  • 15g green tea powder
  • 50g sugar
  • 2g salt
  • 70g melted butter
  • 500ml milk
  • cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Put the eggs in the bowl and loosen it
  2. Sift in cake flour and green tea powder
  3. Add sugar and salt and mix
  4. Add melted butter and mix
  5. Add milk and mix and sieve
  6. Leave in room temperature for 30~60 minutes
  7. Apply oil to the pan and pour batter into pan
  8. Coat the pan with a thin layer of batter and flip crepe when air bubbles come up
  9. After making 16 pieces let it cool
  10. Apply whipped cream on 1 piece of crepe
  11. Stack 4 pieces repeatedly
  12. Then put strawberries on 4th crepe and cover with cream
  13. Repeat steps 2 times
  14. Place the crepe on top of the last layer of strawberries and apply a thin layer of cream
  15. Repeat 3 more times and sprinkle green tea powder on top of the last crepe
  16. Finished :)
