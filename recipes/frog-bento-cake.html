Frog Bento Cake

Description

This is an easy recipe for a frog lunchbox cake that you can make at home!

Ingredients

For the cake base

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cups sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon ovalette
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoon milk

For the frosting

  • 125g unsalted butter
  • 125g vegetable shortening
  • 500g powdered sugar
  • 1/2 tablespoon evaporated milk

Equipment

  • Hand mixer
  • Sieve
  • Oven

Instructions

  1. Mix the eggs, sugar, Ovalette, and vanilla essence in a bowl.
  2. Add sifted all-purpose flour, cocoa powder, and baking powder into the batter and fold the mixture to combine. Then, add 2 tablespoons of oil and milk while mixing.
  3. Pour the batter into 3 different greased 4-inch baking tins and bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes at 180 degrees Celcius. Allow the baked dessert to cool before slicing it into 2 or 3 layers.
  4. For the buttercream, add unsalted butter into a bowl and beat for 2 minutes. Then, add vegetable shortening. Beat for another 3 minutes until it smoothens.
  5. Gradually add sifted powdered sugar and fold the mixture until it thickens. Then, add evaporated milk and vanilla essence before mixing again until the buttercream becomes smooth and creamy.
  6. Assemble the lunchbox cake. Spread buttercream on each layer and apply the first coat of frosting. After 15 minutes, add the final frosting, decorate and serve.
